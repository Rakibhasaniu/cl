SHAMHAN BROMLEY

With the very first Shamhan restaurant started up in Charlton Village, the Bromley location has a very special place in our hearts. It bears a legacy to the very core of how the public took to Shamhan and the food and service it had to offer.

Cosy and with an ambience that is at once warm and intimate, the restaurant boasts the kind of unhurry Indian food that has always been a magnet for locals and those beyond. Hardly surprising that we have guests who swear they would never eat anywhere else!

We keep our menus deceptively simple, yet the dishes are full of flavour and freshly rendered. From traditional items you have come to love from the Indian repertoire together so have won a sizeable following amongst those who are both new to cooking from the subcontinent and those well-versed, spice connoisseurs.

A focal point to our restaurant is the bar from where you can not only order cooling beers, crisp cocktails and mocktails to fine wines and spirits.

SHAMHAN WELLING

Shamhan Welling represents our expansion into the heart of South London, bringing the authentic flavors and warm hospitality that made our Bromley location so beloved. This restaurant showcases our commitment to excellence in every dish.

Located in the vibrant community of Welling, our restaurant features contemporary Indian cuisine prepared with traditional techniques and the finest ingredients. The modern interior design creates an elegant dining atmosphere perfect for any occasion.

Our expert chefs craft each dish with precision, balancing authentic spices and flavors to create memorable culinary experiences. From classic curry dishes to innovative fusion creations, every plate tells a story of culinary excellence.

SHAMHAN WESTERHAM

Our flagship Shamhan Westerham location represents the pinnacle of our culinary journey. This 300-cover restaurant showcases the finest in contemporary Indian dining within the picturesque setting of the Garden of England.

Set in the historic market town of Westerham, our restaurant combines traditional Indian hospitality with modern sophistication. The spacious dining areas and elegant decor create the perfect backdrop for special celebrations and intimate dinners alike.

Our renowned chefs have crafted an extensive menu that celebrates both classic Indian cuisine and innovative contemporary creations. Using only the finest seasonal ingredients, each dish is a masterpiece of flavor, presentation, and culinary artistry.
