Calcutta Fine Indian Dining

With the very first Calcutta Fine Indian Dining restaurant started up in Charlton Village, the Bromley location has a very special place in our hearts. It bears a legacy to the very core of how the public took to Calcutta Fine Indian Dining and the food and service it had to offer.

Cosy and with an ambience that is at once warm and intimate, the restaurant boasts the kind of unhurry Indian food that has always been a magnet for locals and those beyond. Hardly surprising that we have guests who swear they would never eat anywhere else!

We keep our menus deceptively simple, yet the dishes are full of flavour and freshly rendered. From traditional items you have come to love from the Indian repertoire together so have won a sizeable following amongst those who are both new to cooking from the subcontinent and those well-versed, spice connoisseurs.

A focal point to our restaurant is the bar from where you can not only order cooling beers, crisp cocktails and mocktails to fine wines and spirits.
