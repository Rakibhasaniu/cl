OUR STORY

The Calcutta Fine Indian Dining story has very humble beginnings with a group of family members wanting to open an intimate Indian restaurant in Charlton Village, in Bromley, Kent in 1989.

Little did they know at what extent the locals would welcome them with open arms, taking the establishment's delicious cooking, it's warm and friendly environment and personal service to their hearts. The restaurant was such a success that its proven track record made the owners take another bold step by moving the restaurant to a much larger premises a mere stone's throw away, 2002.

With Calcutta Fine Indian Dining becoming such a celebrated household name for food lovers, the next surprise when in 2006 came the opening of Calcutta Fine Indian Dining Welling. The restaurant went from strength to strength and even went on to win a string of awards.

The rest as they say is history and yet for the Calcutta Fine Indian Dining Group, the best was yet to come!

In 2011, Calcutta Fine Indian Dining launched its most ambitious restaurant to date, the 300-cover Calcutta Fine Indian Dining at Spinning Wheel, in Westerham and what has become its flagship restaurant. A brand for comfort, relaxation, flavoursome food, refreshing cocktails and fine dining from a menu that is contemporary whilst paying homage to tradition. Calcutta Fine Indian Dining has proudly been providing excellent service to a county appreciatively known as the Garden of England.
