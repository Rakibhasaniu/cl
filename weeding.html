WEDDINGS & EVENTS

Creating unforgettable memories in elegant settings

EVENTS

From birthday parties to business meetings, baby showers to wedding meals, The Shamhan Group is the ideal venue for your event or celebration. Our mix of classic and contemporary styling provides a range of spaces to suit any occasion.

Whether you want to book out our private dining room for a meeting or hire out the whole venue for your dream wedding, our dedicated team are on hand every step of the way to guide you through your planning.

Our experienced event coordinators work closely with you to ensure every detail is perfectly executed, from menu selection to table arrangements, creating an atmosphere that reflects your vision and exceeds your expectations.

WEDDINGS

The Shamhan Group provides an exclusive-use, luxurious wedding venue. Enjoy arrival drinks in the historic bar area, before taking your guests through to the sophisticated restaurant for your wedding breakfast, which can cater for up to 76.

Come the evening, you may want to make use of our Josper Bar & Grill in our rear courtyard for an informal BBQ for up to 120 guests. Once the day has drawn to a close, you can make use of our 10 boutique bedrooms to avoid the hassle of organising transport.

Our wedding specialists will guide you through every aspect of your special day, from initial planning to the final farewell, ensuring your celebration is as unique and memorable as your love story.
